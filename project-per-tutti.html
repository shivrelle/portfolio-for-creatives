Per Tutti
Italian Restaurant

Modern dining has become rushed, individualistic, and disconnected, leaving people craving the warmth, belonging, and shared wellbeing that food is meant to create.

Unlike most Vancouver Italian restaurants, Per Tutti is a simple space, a long table, and a warm Italian personality: the Miliano family encourages you to dine with your neighbor, and savour meals together like we do at Nonna's house back home.

Packaging
Art Direction
Brand Design

Per Tutti’s brand is crafted to evoke the feeling the Miliano family always wants their customers to feel: one of sitting at Nonna’s table, with golden hour setting the kitchen alight in warm tones as vegetables are chopped, and sauce is stirred, bringing the feeling of being home to life. Intricate patterns on both pasta and sauce packaging evokes rich Italian textiles that are a felt piece of the restaraunt's in-house experience.

For adults with busy working schedules that don't want to eat out all the time, who tries to be mindful about where they spend their money: they can purchase specialty sauces and pastas made with simple but wholesome ingredients.
