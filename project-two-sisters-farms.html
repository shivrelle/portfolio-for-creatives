Two Sisters Farms

The objective of Two Sisters Farms is to grow and sell flowers and vegetables, but also to be a peaceful, bold, and courageous resistance to capitalism by bringing people together over food. They requested a logo for simple labeling in the form of a sticker, which would eventually evolve for social media, a physical sign for a farm stand, and website branding.

Logo Design

Brand Development

Project Overview

The final logo incorporates the sisters as the mountains that inspired their farm name, holding their fresh flower and vegetable harvest together. The final colours were selected to represent that which sustains farms like Two Sisters: water, sunlight, and plant life.
