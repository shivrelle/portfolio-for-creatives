SONUS International Sound Art Festival

SONUS is a celebration of experimental sound art. This project required identity development for a conceptual music festival. The deliverables included three posters spotlighting the distinct personality of three artists. Through illustration and typographic systems, the brand identity invites audiences to engage with sound as a transformative medium.

Copywriting
Branding
Advertising

Sonus engages art and music lovers drawn to experimentation. Its visual identity centers on an abstract, soundwave-inspired logo and a black-and-white website foundation with artist-driven colour accents. Posters and web elements reflect each artist’s style, with interactive features like the transforming “orb” cursor enhancing the personalized, immersive festival experience.

The Sonus Logo acts as the “O” within the word mark. The bitmap aesthetic is a nod to the role of technology in sound art. The standalone symbol alludes to sound waves and echoes: Sound bends, stretches, flows, and fills space which is translated visually into the “orb.” This brings a 3D quality to the graphic elements of the brand. Without it’s depth and shading, the orb shows up as a simple amorphous shape, alluding to the reality that sound can feel expansive and it can also fall flat.
