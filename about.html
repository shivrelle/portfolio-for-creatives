  • About Me

    I’m a Vancouver-based graphic designer and illustrator with a love of dark comedy, colour, and storytelling. I’m driven by an unruly imagination and skilled at communicating — a balance shaped by growing up in Canada but inside a very Irish household. Before becoming a designer, I lived a few different lives: working in tourism, hospitality, vegetable farming, and a brief stint in academia. These experiences make up my “first career,” and gave me a nuanced understanding of people, and the world(s) they live in, but also how to approach creative endeavours with curiosity, care, and spirit.


    My principles for design (& life):

    1. Never give up on play.
    2. Listen well and ask questions.
    3. Start with pencil on paper.
