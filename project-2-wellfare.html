Wellfare

The world of financial education is bland. It is limited in its representation of unique and diverse life experiences and identities. Financial education should be approachable and accessible to everyone.

Wellfare is an online platform for financial education, that caters its content to gender diverse people who identify as queer, and/or who are seeking a non-traditional approach to financial literacy.

Branding
Advertising
Copywriting

Wellfare is for anyone who needs help with personal finance. Maybe they want to feel more in control of their financial situation and manage expenses for the present and future, but struggle to find resources that speak to the relationship between their unique identity and their finances. Or, maybe they want to learn and hope to improve their quality of life, but they also want it to be worth their (valuable) time and feel rewarding too.

Wellfare’s brand employs a sense of humour, honesty, and awareness of its target audience’s desires and language. Logistics, numbers, and structure are inherent in personal finance, but no one ever said it couldn’t also be relatable, colourful or emotive 😉
