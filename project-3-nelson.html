Nelson Brewing:
Winter Ale Label Design

Nelson Brewing Company needed a brand refresh for their organic winter ale. They wanted personality, a distinctive modern look, and an effective promotional piece that would entice audiences to try the new beer.
Every winter beer label within the current market uses minimal colour and a simplistic, geometric design. The brewery wanted slightly more personality, therefore it made sense to create something more playful.

Packaging Redesign
Illustration
Branding

The novelty of a winter beer is captured by Nelly—a playful Abominable Snowperson designed to appeal to younger audiences and stand out on store shelves. The branded merchandise is perfect for small communities like Nelson where word-of-mouth often outperforms traditional advertising.

The Sonus Logo acts as the “O” within the word mark. The bitmap aesthetic is a nod to the role of technology in sound art. The standalone symbol alludes to sound waves and echoes: Sound bends, stretches, flows, and fills space which is translated visually into the “orb.” This brings a 3D quality to the graphic elements of the brand. Without it’s depth and shading, the orb shows up as a simple amorphous shape, alluding to the reality that sound can feel expansive and it can also fall flat.
